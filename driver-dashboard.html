Instructions

  1. Authenticate: Swipe your ID card on the bus-mounted card reader. This will assign you a bus and enable controls on this portal.
  2. Start Trip: Press 'Start Trip' when departing from the depot.
  3. Update Location: Click 'Update Location' as you pass each station (this simulates GPS).
  4. Board Passengers: At the stop, scan student IDs using the input field. The system will verify if they are assigned to your bus and it's their turn.
  5. End Trip: Press 'End Trip' when you arrive at the destination. This updates your daily trip count.
  6. Communicate: Use the 'Send Broadcast' feature to inform passengers.
