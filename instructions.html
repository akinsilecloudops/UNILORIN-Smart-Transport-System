How to Use the UNILORIN Smart Transport System

Follow the steps below based on your role to access and use the system effectively.

For Students

Getting Started & Joining a Queue

1
Login: Go to the login page and select Student as your role. Enter your Matric Number (e.g., 24/30GB001) and password (abdulakeem1234 for demo).
2
Select Terminal: On the dashboard, choose the terminal location you are physically present at (e.g., Main Gate, Tanke).
3
Join Queue: Click the "Join Queue" button. You will be assigned a position in the queue for the next available bus heading towards your destination.
4
Wait & Watch: Your dashboard will show your queue position (e.g., "You are #5") and an estimated waiting time. Keep the app open to receive updates.

Managing Your Wallet

1
Check Balance: Your current wallet balance is displayed on the dashboard.
2
Fund Wallet: Click the "Fund Wallet" button and follow the prompts to add money using the integrated payment option.

Boarding the Bus

1
Wait for Assignment: When it's close to your turn, you will receive a notification that a bus is assigned to you (e.g., "BUS_001 is coming").
2
Present ID: When the bus arrives, the driver will ask you to present your University ID card for scanning.
3
Board & Confirm: If your ID is scanned successfully and it's your turn, the fare will be automatically deducted from your wallet, and you will be allowed to board.
Note: If you attempt to board out of turn, a penalty will be applied, and you will have to wait for multiple subsequent buses before being eligible again.

For Drivers

Driver Authentication

1
Login: On the login page, select Driver as your role. Enter your Driver ID (e.g., DRV001) and password (12345678 for demo).
2
Swipe Card: On the dashboard, click the "Swipe Driver ID Card" button to authenticate yourself and unlock the operational controls.

Operating a Trip

1
Start Trip: Once ready to depart from the depot, click the "Start Trip" button. This begins the journey tracking.
2
Update Location: As you drive along the route, click the "Update Location" button to move the bus icon on the tracking map for passengers.
3
Manage Boarding: At the stop, use the "Scan Student ID" input field to scan the IDs of students wanting to board.
4
Verify & Allow: The system will check if the student is assigned to your bus and it's their turn. If yes, the fare is deducted, and boarding is confirmed. If not, the request is denied.
5
End Trip: When you reach the final destination, click the "End Trip" button to conclude the journey.

Communicating

1
Broadcast Messages: Use the "Broadcast Message" section to send announcements (e.g., delays, route changes) to passengers waiting for your bus.

For Administrators

Monitoring the System

1
Login: Select Admin as your role on the login page. Use Admin ID (e.g., ADM001) and password (000000 for demo).
Dashboard Overview: The main dashboard displays key metrics like the number of active buses, students currently in queues, and active trips. Use the tables to see detailed information about bus locations, queue sizes at terminals, and recent trip logs.
